Middle School

I went to middle school in Kenya where we referred to it as primary school. I went to a school known as Total Care Academy in Nairobi city and studied there from first grade to eighth grade. I graduated from the school with an aggregate grade equivalent to a B+

High School

I also started and completed my high school education in Kenya and I attended a high school known as Aquinas High School which was in Nairobi as well. I graduated high school with an A-, which was equivalent to a GPA of 3.6.

University/College

I studied at Meru University of Science and Technolgy for a year while in Kenya before making plans to study abroad. While I was there I took a bachelor's degree in Computer Security and Forensics. I am currently enrolled at Florida Gulf Coast university, pursuing a bachelor's degree in Software Engineering.
