My name is Samuel Kagwimi Njenga and I am an international student pursuing a degree in Software Engineering, at Florida Gulf Coast University. Learn more about software engineering

I was born and raised in Kenya up until the age of 19, which is when I made plans to study abroad. I learned about Florida Gulf Coast University through my aunt who lives in Fort Myers, the city where the school is located, and after going through the application process I was fortunate enough to be accepted.
